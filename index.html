FAQs


Q) What is a discovery meet
A) Discovery meet is a formal meet and greet session where prospective members will get an overview of YANC

Q) What exactly is YANC, and how is it different from other networking or youth clubs?
A) YANC is a unique networking platform designed to empower young minds through building meaningful connections and learning life skills from young age. Unlike other traditional groups, YANC focuses on experiential learning, mentorship, and personal growth.

Q) How does YANC help young people beyond traditional education?
A) YANC bridges the gap between the traditional education systems and real-world skills by focusing on building life skills, networking, and mentorship opportunities that schools and colleges typically do not offer at scale.

Q) Is YANC only for students, or can working professionals also join?
A) YANC is open to students, young professionals, and entrepreneurs aged 18-28 who are keen on learning, growing, and contributing to a supportive network.

Q) How often do YANC members meet, and what happens in these meetings?
A) YANC members meet typically bi-weekly, to engage in structured discussions,mentorship sessions, networking, and interactive learning experiences.

Q) Is YANC open for members from all backgrounds and fields?
A) Yes! YANC encourages a diverse community where members from various backgrounds and fields can connect and learn from one another.

Q) What is the purpose of an membership application process?
A) The YANC Membership Application Process is designed to identify, evaluate, and onboard passionate young individuals who align with YANC’s mission of empowering young minds through meaningful connections, purposeful learning, and real-world experiences. This structured application process ensures that each applicant is assessed holistically based on their aspirations,skills, leadership potential, and willingness to contribute to the YANC community.

Q) How can someone apply to join YANC?
A)Interested individuals can apply through the official YANC website www.yanc.in,where they will need to fill out 'contact us' form and subsequently will be contacted by YANC on further plan of action.

Q) Is there a selection process? If yes, what does it involve?
A)Yes, YANC has a selection process that includes an application review, an interview, and a short presentation on a given topic to evaluate the applicant's fit.

Q) What happens after someone gets selected as a YANC member??
A)Selected members receive a welcome kit, initiate the onboarding programs (trainings on what YANC is all about and how can be successful ), buddy is assigned to them and are assigned a cohort for ongoing learning and networking activities.

Q) Is there a fee to join YANC? What does it cover?
A)Yes, there is a membership fee that covers the monthly meetups, food & beverage,mentorship sessions, learning resources, and community engagement activities. The fee details will shared as part of discovery meet agenda

Q) Can parents enroll their children directly, or do the youth need to apply themselves?
A)Youth members must apply themselves to ensure commitment and enthusiasm towards YANC’s objectives.

Q) Can students or professionals from any city join, or is YANC limited to specific locations?
A) For now, YANC is based out of Hyderabad city only. Applicants need to be located in Hyderabad in order to be part of YANC. YANC will be expanding to multiple cities in the imminent future.

Q) If someone is not selected, can they reapply later?
A) Yes, applicants who are not selected can reapply after a certain period and are encouraged to continue their self-development in the meantime.

Q) When is the payment due after selection?
A) Once an applicant is selected, a payment link will be shared and they must complete their membership payment within one week to confirm their spot.

Q) What is the cost of YANC membership?
A) The membership fee is Rs. 4500 per month, payable yearly.

Q) What payment methods are accepted?
A) YANC accepts online payments via credit/debit cards, UPI, bank transfers, and digital wallets.

Q) Are there any refunds if someone decides to discontinue?
A)Answer: No, the membership fee is non-refundable. However, in special cases, deferment to the next cohort may be considered..

Q) Does the membership fee cover all YANC events and programs?
A)The fee includes access to regular monthly bi-weekly bi-weekly meetings, mentorship sessions, learning materials, networking opportunities. If there are any off sites, socials,special events, the cost will be additional and will shared event to event basis. It is upto members discretion to either participate in the additional event or not.

Q) What is the objective of Young Minds Mashup funding process?
A) To establish a structured and transparent process for evaluating, approving, and monitoring the funding of prototype development projects under the YANC Prototype Fund in partnership with Sri Venture Partners (SVP). This program aims to provide financial support and mentorship to young entrepreneurs aged 15 to 28, fostering innovation and enabling the transition from idea to prototype.
